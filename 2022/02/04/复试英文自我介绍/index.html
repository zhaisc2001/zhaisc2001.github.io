复试英文自我介绍

2022-02-04 technology PV:

Good afternoon, dear teachers! It is my great honor to have this opportunity for this interview. My name is Xiangweixi. I come from Sichuan province and Chengdu city. During my undergraduate years in UESTC, I majored in e-commerce and minored in electronic engineering. I will finish my undergraduate study in June, 2022. Now please allow me to introduce myself from the aspects of learning experience, scientific research, application motivation and planning.
First of all, during my undergraduate study, I directly communicated with professors, participated in the research of the subject named Unsupervised learning-based EEG high-frequency oscillation signal detection and localization of epileptogenic foci, and published the paper of A NOVEL UNSUPERVISED AUTOENCODER-BASED HFOS DETECTOR IN INTRACRANIAL EEG SIGNALS, which was accepted by IEEE ICASSP 2022. This research experience helped me to improve my practical ability and experimental skills, deepen my knowledge of deep learning, and inspire my future research ideas.
Secondly, in my spare time, I am a member of Worshop of Innovation and Entrepreneurship, I have participated in some forum activities on management and made some reports related to data science. These experiences have broadened my horizon, trained my practical skills and made me think about what kind of research I will carry out in the future.
Finally, from my point of view, I like management science and engeering major of UESTC very much, and I have paid attention to it in my spare time. The reason why I choose to take the postgraduate examination is that I want to further improve my professional level, deepen my understanding of the major . Besides, the demand for highly educated students in the current society is also increasing, and graduate students can adapt to the present society with a better attitude.
If I am lucky enough to get a master’s degree in UESTC, I will study harder, being strict with myself and improving my abilities in all aspects. Specifically, I will study hard and master all courses in the first year of graduate school to make up for the lack of professional knowledge. In the second year of postgraduate study, I will collect materials to prepare the thesis proposal,actively participating in various experiments, academic seminars, practical research activities, etc., to promote the combination of theory and practice, and constantly improve theoretical literacy and practical ability. Finally, I will finish my graduation thesis in the third year and prepare for graduation defense. If my research ability is recognized by my supervisor during the postgraduate period, I will continue my doctoral study. In short, in my future study career, I will continue to polish myself, make myself more calm and devote myself to study and research. It is going to be a learning process that I have been looking forward to for a long time and I have put all my efforts into it. I have prepared myself well.
The above is my self-introduction, and thank you for listening.
